01 — About

The person behind

I'm Nicole Jones — a career-transitioning QA professional combining 20+ years of compliance, process improvement, and system accuracy experience with hands-on technical training in software quality assurance, API testing, and AI automation.

My background in high-stakes environments — healthcare, behavioral health, and government human services — gave me an obsession with accuracy, structured thinking, and finding the gap between what a system is supposed to do and what it actually does.

I specialize in the intersection of quality engineering and AI automation — applying tools like n8n, Make.com, and custom AI agents to create workflows that are precise, reliable, and built to last.

Holding an MS in Counseling Psychology from Troy State University, an MPA from Columbus State University, and a Dual Bachelor of Arts in Sociology and Political Science from the University of West Georgia, I bring a systems-thinking mindset to every problem — understanding not just the technical stack, but the human and organizational layers that make automation actually stick.
